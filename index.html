How many electrons can the first energy level (s) of an atom hold according to the Aufbau principle?

Describe the Bohr model of the atom.

The Bohr model describes electrons orbiting the nucleus in fixed energy levels or shells.

What is the difference between covalent and ionic bonding?

Covalent bonding involves the sharing of electrons between atoms, while ionic bonding involves the transfer of electrons from one atom to another.

What is the molecular formula for methane?

How do you determine the number of valence electrons in an atom?

The number of valence electrons in an atom is determined by its group number in the periodic table.
